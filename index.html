WINTER EVEREST MARATHON: CELEBRATING HEIGHTS

Join us in honoring the new peak elevation of Mount Everest at 8848.86 m, with the Winter Everest Marathon.

Every two years, runners from around the world converge at Mt. Everest Base Camp to partake in this high-altitude adventure, choosing between the challenging

42KM FULL MARATHON AND THE EXHILARATING 21KM HALF MARATHON.

More than just a race, it's an unparalleled experience that transcends boundaries and showcases the indomitable spirit of humanity.

As Edmund Hillary once said, "It is not the mountain we conquer, but ourselves."
